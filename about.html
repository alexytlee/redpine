About Red Pine

Opening up crypto for everyone with compliance and accessibility

Our Vision

Red Pine is being built to make cryptocurrency accessible to everyone. We believe the future of digital assets should be open, secure, and compliant. By simplifying access to staking, crypto-native products, and trading, we're opening up the entire crypto ecosystem for the masses.

Our Mission

To democratize access to digital assets by building a secure and compliant platform from the ground up. We're enabling everyone to participate in staking, DeFi, and crypto-native products while meeting Hong Kong's regulatory standards.

Why Hong Kong

We are Hong Kongers who are passionate about the digital asset industry. Hong Kong's progressive regulatory framework and position as a global financial hub make it an ideal location for a compliant digital asset exchange. We are building Red Pine to contribute to Hong Kong's digital asset ecosystem.
