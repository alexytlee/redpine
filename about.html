About Red Pine

Building the future of digital asset trading in Hong Kong

Our Vision

Red Pine is being developed to provide a secure, compliant, and user-friendly platform for digital asset trading in Hong Kong. We are committed to meeting the highest standards of regulatory compliance and security.

Our Mission

To create a trusted digital asset exchange that serves both retail and institutional clients with professional-grade infrastructure, robust security measures, and exceptional service.

Why Hong Kong

We are Hong Kongers who are passionate about the digital asset industry. Hong Kong's progressive regulatory framework and position as a global financial hub make it an ideal location for a compliant digital asset exchange. We are building Red Pine to contribute to Hong Kong's digital asset ecosystem.
